# Artifacts

Recommended outputs from this skill:

## 1. Current-state snapshot

Document:

- current artifacts
- team roles and interactions
- conventions in active use
- major constraints
- major unknowns

## 2. Gap register

Document:

- missing durable rules
- unclear ownership
- inconsistent documentation or quality expectations
- risks to Route and Specify

## 3. Draft platform baseline

Document:

- draft principles or constitution
- draft reusable config context
- draft platform versioning and durable ref model
- draft JIRA hierarchy and issue-link conventions
- role and interaction map
- adoption notes

## 4. Follow-up list

Document:

- open questions
- items to validate with teams
- platform items to refine after first pilots
