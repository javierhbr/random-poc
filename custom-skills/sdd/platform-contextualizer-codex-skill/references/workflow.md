# Workflow

Use this workflow for an existing platform.

```text
Step 1
[Observe current platform]
  repos, docs, artifacts, workflows, team habits
        |
        v
Step 2
[Document current state]
  facts, constraints, conventions, ownership, unknowns
        |
        v
Step 3
[Identify gaps]
  missing rules, unclear ownership, inconsistent practices
        |
        v
Step 4
[Draft platform baseline]
  principles, config, role map, quality language,
  versioning, component alignment, JIRA conventions
        |
        v
Step 5
[Validate with teams]
  confirm, correct, simplify, adopt
```

This skill prepares the platform for the Platform phase of the unified SDD
workflow. It does not replace the Platform phase. It makes it practical.
