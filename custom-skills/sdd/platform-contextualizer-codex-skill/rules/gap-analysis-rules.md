# Gap Analysis Rules

Use these rules when identifying gaps between the current platform and the
desired Platform-phase baseline.

## Goal

Identify the most important gaps and areas for improvement without turning the
exercise into a perfection project.

## Must do

- compare current state against Platform-phase outcomes
- identify missing principles, unclear ownership, missing config, and inconsistent quality expectations
- prioritize gaps by impact on future Route and Specify work
- call out where teams disagree or follow different conventions

## Avoid

- listing every minor inconsistency
- hiding risk behind generic wording
- proposing too many changes at once

## Required outputs

- prioritized gap register
- risk notes
- recommended next actions
